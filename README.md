Hackathon Rules
## Instructions for Participants
Participants must submit their project by creating a new issue in this repository.

### Steps to Submit:
1. Go to the **Issues** tab at the top of this repository.
2. Click on the **New issue** button.
3. Set the title as `Submission - [Your Team Name/Project Name]`.
4. In the body of the issue, include:
   - A link to your code.
   - A link to your demo video (Google Drive or YouTube).
5. Click **Submit new issue**.

Ensure your video link is accessible.
